# Persona Profile Template

Use this template when creating simulated user personas during Phase 2 of the UX review.

## Profile Structure

```markdown
# Persona: [Name]

## Demographics
- **Role/Title:** [e.g., Small Business Owner, Senior Developer, First-time User]
- **Age Range:** [e.g., 25-34]
- **Tech Comfort Level:** [Novice / Intermediate / Advanced / Expert]
- **Industry/Domain:** [e.g., Healthcare, E-commerce, Education]

## Goals & Motivations
- **Primary Goal:** [What they most want to accomplish with this app]
- **Secondary Goals:** [Other things they'd like to achieve]
- **Underlying Motivation:** [Why these goals matter to them]

## Context & Environment
- **Typical Workflow:** [How they currently accomplish their tasks]
- **Devices Used:** [Desktop, mobile, tablet — and in what contexts]
- **Time Constraints:** [How much time they typically have for this task]
- **Frequency of Use:** [Daily, weekly, occasionally]

## Pain Points & Frustrations
- **Current Frustrations:** [What bothers them about existing solutions]
- **Accessibility Needs:** [Any specific accessibility requirements]
- **Common Mistakes:** [Errors they're likely to make]

## Success Criteria
- **Task Completion:** [What "done" looks like for them]
- **Time Expectation:** [How long they expect the task to take]
- **Satisfaction Indicators:** [What would make them recommend this app]

## Key Questions This Persona Would Ask
1. [Question reflecting their primary concern]
2. [Question about their workflow fit]
3. [Question about learning curve or onboarding]
4. [Question about reliability or trust]
```

## Persona Diversity Guidelines

When generating personas, ensure coverage across:

- **Tech comfort levels:** Include at least one novice and one expert
- **Use frequency:** Mix daily power users with occasional visitors
- **Accessibility:** Include at least one persona with accessibility needs
- **Goals:** Vary primary goals to cover different app workflows
- **Context:** Mix desktop-first and mobile-first users where relevant

## Example Persona

```markdown
# Persona: Sarah Chen

## Demographics
- **Role/Title:** Marketing Manager at a mid-size SaaS company
- **Age Range:** 30-39
- **Tech Comfort Level:** Intermediate
- **Industry/Domain:** B2B Software Marketing

## Goals & Motivations
- **Primary Goal:** Quickly generate campaign performance reports for stakeholders
- **Secondary Goals:** Identify underperforming channels, export data for presentations
- **Underlying Motivation:** Needs to justify marketing spend to leadership quarterly

## Context & Environment
- **Typical Workflow:** Checks dashboards Monday morning, builds reports Friday afternoon
- **Devices Used:** Laptop at office, tablet during meetings for quick checks
- **Time Constraints:** 30 minutes max for report generation
- **Frequency of Use:** 3-4 times per week

## Pain Points & Frustrations
- **Current Frustrations:** Too many clicks to get to the data she needs
- **Accessibility Needs:** None specific
- **Common Mistakes:** Selects wrong date range, forgets to apply filters

## Success Criteria
- **Task Completion:** PDF report with key metrics ready to share
- **Time Expectation:** Under 10 minutes for a standard weekly report
- **Satisfaction Indicators:** "I can get my report without asking engineering for help"

## Key Questions This Persona Would Ask
1. "Can I save my report configuration so I don't have to set it up every time?"
2. "How do I compare this month's performance to last month?"
3. "Can I share this dashboard link with my VP without them needing an account?"
4. "What happens if I accidentally delete a saved report?"
```
